GT Cars

Our goal is to create a game with different mechanisms but also to change the user interface. With the current situation we are in where everyone is at home and spends most of their time on their computers, we decided to head to a game made for computer platforms. With the elegance of the game the graphics create the feeling of unforgettable and pleasant time of dedication.

The objective of the game is for the player to reach the end of the finish line as well as to collect coins and key, but the player has to be careful while going through the track, many obstacles will appear to slow him down as well as to stop him from finishing. The player will purchase different cars from the coins he collected so he can outrun the police.
